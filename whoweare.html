The Mainland Pantry

A Community Food Bank in Mainland, MI.

The Mainland Pantry was created to support people facing food insecurity. We believe every person deserves access to healthy food, kindness, and community support.

Our pantry provides groceries, fresh produce, nutrition resources, disaster relief support, and outreach programs for youth, adults, and families.
